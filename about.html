How it started

Kape Kayumanggi started with a broke scholar who had a great love for coffee and money. The business was only just a stall in the streets of Pampanga, selling freshly brewed cups of coffee and repackaged roasted coffee beans.




How it's going now

We now own imaginary walk-in stores throughout many imaginary plazas and malls. Kape Kayumanggi still stayed to its roots, selling warms cups of coffee. But now, due to capitalism, the store has adapted trendy foods like matcha and Japanese-inspired rice bowls!
